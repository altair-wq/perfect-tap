## Privacy policy (required)

Generate a free one at:
- https://app-privacy-policy-generator.firebaseapp.com/
- https://www.termly.io/

Disclose:
- AdMob collects device identifiers for ad personalization
- localStorage stores high score on device only
- No data sent to your servers

Host on your domain at `/privacy`. Both stores require a live URL.

## Apple "Privacy Nutrition Label"
For a v1 with only AdMob ads:
- **Data Used to Track You**: Identifiers (Device ID), Usage Data (Product Interaction), Diagnostics (Crash data, Performance data)
- **Data Linked to You**: None (you don't have accounts)
- **Data Not Linked to You**: Same as "Used to Track" above

Apple's wording is confusing. The AdMob docs have an exact checklist:
https://developers.google.com/admob/ios/privacy/manifest

## Screenshots — what to show

You get 5–10 screenshots per platform. Order matters (1st two are what users see in search results).

1. **Hero shot**: gameplay mid-perfect, big "PERFECT" text, high score visible. Add a tagline overlay like "TAP IN THE ZONE."
2. **Combo shot**: x15 combo visible, score 200+. Tagline: "BUILD STREAKS."
3. **Difficulty curve**: fast indicator blurred, tagline "IT GETS FASTER."
4. **Game over**: new best score celebration. Tagline: "BEAT YOUR BEST."
5. **Menu screen**: clean, branded.

Use Figma or fastlane snapshot to overlay text on real device screenshots.
